# Configure notifications

As a recipe author, you will be notified if your recipe experiences errors or exceptions while running. You will also receive a notification when the recipe is going to expire as a reminder to extend the expiration if the recipe is still useful. To configure which notifications you receive and how you receive them, navigate to your Foundry user settings from the bottom of the navigation sidebar.

![Open Foundry user settings](/docs/resources/foundry/recipes/configure_run_part1.png)

Click the **Notifications** tab under **User settings**, find the **Recipes** section, and choose whether you want to receive notifications by email, web, or none at all.

![Choose Recipe notification preferences](/docs/resources/foundry/recipes/configure_run_part2.png)
