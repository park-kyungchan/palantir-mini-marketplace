# Cross-application interactivity

Workshop supports all methods of cross-application interactivity to enable data sharing across distinct applications.

Use [drag and drop](/docs/foundry/cross-app-interactivity/drag-and-drop-overview/) and the [App Pairing widget](/docs/foundry/cross-app-interactivity/app-pairing/) to integrate your Workshop module with and connect it to other Palantir applications. [Commands](/docs/foundry/cross-app-interactivity/commands-overview/) enable you to execute actions and operations across applications independent of or in tandem with the App Pairing widget.

For Workshop-specific guidance on configuring drag zones and drop zones, see [Drag and drop in Workshop](/docs/foundry/workshop/drag-and-drop/).

[Learn more about cross-application interactivity](/docs/foundry/cross-app-interactivity/overview/).
