# Show and hide content

:::callout{theme="neutral"}
This will affect how all editors and viewers see the report.
:::

## Show / hide the report creator

To show or hide the name of the user who created a report:

1. *(If needed)* Switch to Editing mode.

2. Click the **Settings** button in the application header. <br><br>
   ![Settings button in header](/docs/resources/foundry/reports/settings-button-in-header-1.png) <br><br>

3. Click **Show creator name** in the settings menu. <br><br>
   ![Show creator name](/docs/resources/foundry/reports/show-creator-name-menu-item.png) <br><br>

As a report editor, you can opt to show or hide the creator name above the report title. <br><br>
![Show creator name example](/docs/resources/foundry/reports/show-creator-name-example.gif) <br><br>

## Show / hide the report outline

To show or hide the outline in a report:

1. *(If needed)* Switch to Editing mode.

2. Click the **Actions** button in the application header. <br><br>
   ![Actions button in header](/docs/resources/foundry/reports/actions-button-in-header.png) <br><br>

3. Click **Show outline** in the actions menu. <br><br>
   ![Show outline action button](/docs/resources/foundry/reports/show-outline-action-button.png) <br><br>

This will toggle the outline button in the top-right of the report:

![Outline example](/docs/resources/foundry/reports/outline-example.gif)

## Show / hide a piece of content's title

To show or hide the title of a piece of content:

1. *(If needed)* Switch to Editing mode.

2. Click the **Settings** cog in top right corner of a piece of content <br><br>
   ![Widget settings button](/docs/resources/foundry/reports/widget-settings-cog.png) <br><br>

3. Click **Show title** in the settings menu. <br><br>
   ![Show title menu item](/docs/resources/foundry/reports/widget-settings-show-title.png) <br><br>

This will toggle the title above the widget.

![Widget settings show title example](/docs/resources/foundry/reports/widget-settings-show-hide-title.gif)

:::callout{theme="neutral"}
Content titles are displayed in the report outline in order to improve navigability.
:::

## Show / hide a piece of content's description

To show or hide the title of a piece of content:

1. *(If needed)* Switch to Editing mode.

2. Click the Settings cog in top right corner of a piece of content <br><br>
   ![Widget settings button](/docs/resources/foundry/reports/widget-settings-cog.png) <br><br>

3. Click **Show description** in the settings menu. <br><br>
   ![Show description menu item](/docs/resources/foundry/reports/widget-settings-show-description.png) <br><br>

This will toggle the description below the widget.

![Widget settings show description example](/docs/resources/foundry/reports/widget-settings-show-hide-description.gif)

## Display parameters in a toolbar or sidebar

Reports lets you control how report parameters are displayed:

Display parameters in a *sidebar* if you have many parameters and want to display them in a scrollable list.

![Parameter in sidebar](/docs/resources/foundry/reports/parameters-sidebar.png)

Display parameters in a *toolbar* (along the top) if you only have a few parameters and/or if the report content needs more width to display optimally.

![Parameter in toolbar](/docs/resources/foundry/reports/parameters-toolbar.png)

To change how the list of parameters is displayed:

1. Click **Settings** in the application header. <br><br>
   ![Settings button in header](/docs/resources/foundry/reports/settings-button-in-header-1.png) <br><br>

2. Under "Parameters Display", click **Sidebar** or **Toolbar** as needed. <br><br>
   ![Toggle between sidebar or toolbar](/docs/resources/foundry/reports/show-parameters-sidebar-toolbar.gif) <br><br>
