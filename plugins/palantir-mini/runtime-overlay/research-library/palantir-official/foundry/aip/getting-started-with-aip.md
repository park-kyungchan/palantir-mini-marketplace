# Get started with AIP

To help you understand the conceptual basics of Foundry and AIP, we recommend that you study the relevant educational content on the Palantir Learning portal at [learn.palantir.com ↗](https://learn.palantir.com). This includes:

1. [Introduction to Foundry & AIP for Enterprise Organizations ↗](http://learn.palantir.com/introduction-to-foundry-aip-for-enterprise-organizations): This course is designed to provide you with a foundational understanding of the platform and Palantir's approach to evolving data management systems.

2. [Scoping Use Cases for Foundry & AIP ↗](https://learn.palantir.com/scoping-use-cases-for-foundry-aip): This short 15-minute course covers how to scope and prioritize use cases in the platform.

3. [Speedrun: Your First AIP Workflow ↗](https://learn.palantir.com/speedrun-your-e2e-aip-workflow): This hands-on course takes you through the process of building an AI assistant with AIP in 60-90 minutes. The course covers how to extract information from PDFs, incorporate the data into the Ontology, configure an AIP Chatbot (formerly known as an AIP Agent) based on your knowledge graph, and build an interactive application to work with your chatbot.
