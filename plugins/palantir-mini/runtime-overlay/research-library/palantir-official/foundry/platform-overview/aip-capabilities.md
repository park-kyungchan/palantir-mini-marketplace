# AIP capabilities

Palantir AIP (Artificial Intelligence Platform) provides a full set of capabilities for building with generative AI and connecting AI to operations. This includes governed access to a wide range of LLMs and multimodal models, building LLM-driven functions, creating and managing agents, managing the evaluation suites that measure AI performance, defining the automations that will run across enterprise processes, and more.

AIP streamlines the deployment, management, and scalability of LLMs while ensuring robust data governance and security. Beyond standalone application builder experiences, AIP transforms the [development toolchain](/docs/foundry/dev-toolchain/overview/), enabling LLMs and other forms of generative AI to power ontology-driven functions and agents.

Learn more about AIP in the [AIP documentation](/docs/foundry/aip/overview/).
