# Increasing client engagement through integrated campaign management

> Industry Sector: **Health Care**
>
> Business Function: **Customer Relations**

Palantir Foundry can be used to segment members based on specific characteristics, orchestrate the end-to-end campaign management lifecycle, and propose next best actions according to the member's risk profile and responses. One campaign sent over 100M messages to members leading to a 1.6X increase in vaccination rates.

## Challenge

Member engagement was managed within markets to account for state- and product-level restrictions, leading to an inability to deploy enterprise-wide engagement strategies or learn and improve on best practices.

## Solution

**Dynamic targeting across member characteristics:** fully integrated Member view enables dynamic targeting based on demographics, medical claims, prescriptions, socioeconomic factors, etc. to identify member cohorts for engagement.

**End-to-end campaign management:** coordination of multi-channel campaigns with progress tracking against specific success KPIs and member behavior targets to inform future campaign strategies.

**Next Best Action proposals:** proactively surface opportunities for engagement based on claims, electronic health records, and Rx adherence, among other inputs.

### Users and stakeholders

* Member Orchestration

## Impact

* Over 100M messages sent in first enterprise-wide campaign.
* 1.6X increase in COVID-19 vaccination rate for contacted members.
