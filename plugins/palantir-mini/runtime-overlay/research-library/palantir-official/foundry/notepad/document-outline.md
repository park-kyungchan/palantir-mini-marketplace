# Document outline

You can view your document's outline in the left panel. The outline is automatically generated from your document's headings, and selecting an outline item will take you to the respective section. You can also copy an anchor link to a heading by selecting the anchor option in the outline.

![Outline section of a Notepad document.](/docs/resources/foundry/notepad/notepad_outline.png)

## Embedded outline

To view your document's outline in other applications, such as in Workshop, you need to toggle the **Show outline when embedded** option from the view menu.

![Embedded outline shown to the right of the Notepad document.](/docs/resources/foundry/notepad/show_embedded_outline.png)
