# Authentication

## SAML integration

Apollo supports integration with your organization’s SAML provider of choice. Apollo accepts your standard federation metadata and may ask for some clarification regarding the claim types in use by your identity provider (IdP). Palantir will supply your IdP with Palantir's service provider metadata.

## Palantir Auth

If you do not wish to login to the platform via your SAML provider or do not have a SAML provider, Palantir can provide your users with logins via Palantir Auth, an external IdP. Contact your Palantir representative to learn more.
