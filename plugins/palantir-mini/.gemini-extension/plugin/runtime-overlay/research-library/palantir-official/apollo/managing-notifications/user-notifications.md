# User notifications

This section will walk through how to receive in-app and email notifications for a resource.

## Enable notifications for a resource

You can subscribe to both in-app and email notifications for a resource. Email notifications will automatically go to your linked email address. To enable notifications for a resource:

1. Navigate to a resource page, such as an Environment, Product, or team.
2. Find the actions icon on the top right and toggle on in-app or email notifications for yourself. <br><br>
   ![Opt-in to notifications user after](/docs/resources/apollo/managing-notifications/opt-in-product-notifs-after.png) <br><br>
3. Customize your notification preferences for this resource in settings. This will determine what [notification types](/docs/apollo/managing-notifications/overview/#notification-types) you will receive. <br><br>
   ![Customize notification types](/docs/resources/apollo/managing-notifications/customize-notifs-user.png) <br><br>
   Select notification types to receive and save your preferences.
4. To receive notifications for all future notification types, use the toggle to subscribe to all notifications for that resource. <br><br>
   ![Subscribe to all notifications](/docs/resources/apollo/managing-notifications/subscribe-all-notifs.png) <br><br>
5. In a resource's settings page, team administrators can subscribe contacts to specific resources. This is useful for resources that do not have contact teams. <br><br>
   ![Add team contact](/docs/resources/apollo/managing-notifications/subscribe-team-contact.png) <br><br>
   New contacts cannot be created on a resource. To add additional contacts, open Hub Settings for that team.

## Disable notifications

There are two ways to unsubscribe from notifications for a resource:

1. Notifications pop-over: Open the menu on the top right of each resource. Opt-out of each resource.
2. Go to Hub settings and view all the resources you are subscribed to.
