## HITL Pattern

At a user feedback point, the artifact should show the next session decision,
not only the content under review.

The useful choices are:

- Continue inside the approved boundary.
- Revise the meaning or boundary.
- Hold before mutation.
- Start a fresh session with a handoff.
- Delegate verification to a subagent.
